2.1.1 Variables:

Tiga jenis assignment dalam Ruby:
1. simple
  x = 3
  y = "Hello Ruby!"

2. abbreviated
  x += 1 --> x = x + 1

3. parallel
  x,y,z = 1,2,3


Variabel di ruby:
  1. No declaration
  2. Dynamic typing

Konvensi di ruby:
  1. snake_case
  2. named after meaning of the data
  3. not abbreviated
